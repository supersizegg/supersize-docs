Slimecoin.io is the player-facing app for the Slimecoin protocol. It turns on-chain game sessions, wallet balances, matchmaking queues, rewards, and leaderboards into a fast arcade experience.

## What players see

Players see:

- a wallet-connected arcade lobby
- free-play and paid game modes
- USDC, SLIME, and Slimecoin balances
- leagues, quests, tournament surfaces, and profile progression
- result overlays that explain winnings, opponent outcomes, and claimable rewards

## What the protocol does underneath

Behind the UI, each game session is backed by protocol accounts:

1. The vault profile stores the player's balances and session authority.
2. The game program creates or resumes a game session PDA.
3. Matchmaking either pairs the player or queues their score/seed.
4. The vault settles buy-ins, winnings, fees, Slimecoin, SLIME, leagues, and payouts.

## Why it matters

The player experience is meant to feel simple, but the settlement model remains explicit. A match result is not just an app screen; it is the result of game-owned session state, matchmaking-owned queue state, and vault-owned balance accounting.

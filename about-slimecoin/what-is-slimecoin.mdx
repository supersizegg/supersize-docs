---

Slimecoin is the protocol's play-to-mine token. It is emitted through real gameplay activity rather than minted on a fixed time schedule.

## The basic idea

When players compete in paid USD matches, the vault credits Slimecoin rewards based on the buy-in and current halving level:

- winners mine a higher reward per USD
- losers mine a lower participation reward per USD
- emissions halve as the remaining mineable supply crosses supply thresholds

At the base emission level, a paid $1 buy-in awards 50 Slimecoin to the winner and 5 Slimecoin to the loser.

## What Slimecoin is not

Slimecoin is not the same as SLIME.

| Asset | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Slimecoin | Play-to-mine reward token and league ranking asset |
| SLIME | USDC-backed free-play currency used for SLIME queues and claims |

For the full emission math, supply, and halving table, see [Slimecoin](/tokens/slimecoin).

Slime szn 1 is the first documented seasonal loop for Slimecoin gameplay.

The protocol season state is tracked through vault ledgers. When a new season or week is set, rollup ledgers reset weekly accounting and players start building new activity toward Slime Rush and leagues.

## What resets

At season rollover, the player weekly path resets:

- weekly volume
- weekly quest milestone bits
- SLIME boost multiplier
- leagues season eligibility

## What carries forward

Longer-term player state remains:

- profile ownership and session authority
- USDC, SLIME, and Slimecoin balances
- lifetime buy-ins, winnings, and platform earnings
- claimed Slimecoin progress

## Why seasons exist

Seasonal accounting gives the protocol clean reward windows. It lets Slime Rush, weekly leagues, and reward pools reset without erasing player-owned balances.

For the mechanics behind weekly pools and claims, see [Leagues](/rewards/leagues) and [Slime Rush](/rewards/slime-rush).
